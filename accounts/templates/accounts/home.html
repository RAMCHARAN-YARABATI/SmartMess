Instructions

  • To book meals, go to the "Orders" tab and select your desired meal slots.
  • You can book Breakfast, Lunch, or Dinner for today or tomorrow, depending on the available time window.
  • Each booking will generate a unique QR code for verification during meal pickup.
  • Meals must be booked before the respective cutoff time (e.g., Lunch by 9:00 AM).
  • If you cancel a booking before the cutoff, the amount will be refunded to your wallet.
  • If you missed the booking window, you can try to order a meal on the "Special Orders" page, which has limited, first-come, first-served slots for the current day.
  • To transfer a booked meal to another student, use the "Exchange QR" feature. The student receiving the meal will receive an OTP for confirmation.
